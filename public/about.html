About Me

Over the past 40 years I have had various careers in wildly different field such as medical physics, health, airlines, hospitality and operations management. Now it is time, once again for me to make a career change to keep myself challenged.

In high-school in the early 80's paleolithic period I learned to program in BASIC and had a lot of fun with it.

In the 90’s I started learning html, when websites were still very basic and everything was new. I found it quite enjoyable but, at the time, I didn’t see myself making a career out of it (oh! if I only knew then!). Work commitments at the time meant that I was unable to pursue my learning as I focused on my current career and I left website design behind.

Recently I left a career that was no longer exciting me and was just becoming tedious and draining. After some time off and speaking to some younger friends working in the field I decided it might be time to revisit the possibility of a career in web development.

I really enjoy travel so having a career that can "travel with me" would be awesome.

I’m sure that the learning curve will be quite steep but I do love giving myself new challenges so this is the first step.
